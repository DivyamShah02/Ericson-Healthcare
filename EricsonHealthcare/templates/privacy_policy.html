Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 22, 2025

This Privacy Policy outlines how Ericson Healthcare (“we,” “our,” or “us”) handles the information of employees who use our in-house application (“App”) to assist with claim investigations for health insurance companies. The App is strictly for internal use and subject to employee agreements signed upon joining.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information to facilitate case investigations and ensure operational efficiency:

  • Personal identification information (e.g., employee ID, name, department).
  • Case-related data (e.g., claim details, investigation notes).
  • Device information (e.g., IP address, usage logs).

2. How We Use the Information

The information collected is used for the following purposes:

  • Facilitating claim investigations and processing.
  • Monitoring and improving operational efficiency.
  • Ensuring compliance with company policies and legal obligations.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect the data stored within the App. Only authorized employees with proper credentials can access sensitive information. Breaches or misuse of data may result in disciplinary actions as per company policy.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share employee data or case-related information with third parties unless required by law or for compliance with contractual obligations.

5. Employee Responsibilities

Employees are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of the data accessed through the App. Unauthorized use or sharing of information is strictly prohibited and subject to company policies.

6. Updates to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in practices or legal requirements. Employees will be notified of any significant changes through official company channels.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the team at contact@ericsonhealthcare.com.

By using the App, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
